Grounded HLC

G

Grounded Holistic Lifestyle Co. Online Shop

Brief:

The Grounded HLC eCommerce project was littered with exciting learning opportunities. It is fullstack MERN and utilizes Stripe for the payment processing. The two largest hurdles to overcome were Stripe, and CORS. I was unfamiliar with the complexity of either of these topics at the start of this project, I am now much more familiar with what 'headers' are. Overall, a project that both myself and the client are very happy with. And it works! Which is just great.

Grounded Holistic Lifestyle Co.
Technologies Used:

React Hooks, SASS, MongoDB, Stripe, Next.js, JWT, Cloudinary, HTML5/CSS3
