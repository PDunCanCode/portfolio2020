Apaullo

A

A Serverless Music Player.

Brief:

Apaullo is a serverless music player designed with the developer in mind. I like many other developers got distracted by my long form coding music mixes being interupted by commercials. And built out Apaullo as a solution to pesky interupting commercials. A user can add a link to any Soundcloud or Youtube video and Apaullo will play the audio from that link uninterupted and commercial free.
